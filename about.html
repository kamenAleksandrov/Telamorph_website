About Telamorph

We're not just a manufacturer — we're the engineering brain behind the product.

Telamorph was founded on a simple idea: the people who design a product should also be the ones who build it. We combine deep engineering expertise with in-house manufacturing to deliver products across automotive, astronomy, and industrial sectors — from concept to production.

Our capabilities include 3D scanning, reverse engineering, CNC machining, carbon fiber layup, and precision assembly. Every product we ship is designed, tested, and refined under one roof.
