About Telamorph

Founded by Yanislav and Svetlozar, two aeronautical design engineers based in Sofia, Bulgaria, Telamorph brings engineering and manufacturing together under one roof. Behind every project stands a team of people who care about their part of the process — design, simulation, tooling, production, finishing, and assembly.

What connects us is the same mindset: to build products properly, with clear purpose, technical discipline, and attention to detail. From automotive and motorsport to aeronautical and industrial applications, we combine proven engineering principles with modern technology to deliver lightweight, reliable, and high-quality composite products.
